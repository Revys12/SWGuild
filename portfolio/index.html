About Me

Guitarist // Producer // Song-Writer

I'm a mixed bag of formally schooled and self-taught as a song-writer, which is an odd combination for most musicians today. But, crossing boundaries and mixing ideas that wouldn't normally don't go together is my favorite thing about art.

Working with me is an endless game of experimentation. Let's take an idea, turn it on it's head, and then try it out 5 different ways. The best version, or at least, the one that feels the most accurate to the moment will reveal itself in the process. The more I've been in music, the more I've been feeling that I don't write songs as much as I learn to discover them.
