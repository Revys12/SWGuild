Since its doors first opened in 1999, Lake has quickly grown to be a staple of the Northwest Seattle neighborhoods. A single-room brewery turned full-scale restaurant, the drive for distinct flavor that started with the first batch of the Cedar Cabin Stout has defined the process for selecting each item on the menu. Combining American classics with Italian heritage, the flavors are bold and interpretetations unparalleled. Stop in for a Seattle taste you can't get anywhere else.
